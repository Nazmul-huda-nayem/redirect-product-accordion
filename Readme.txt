== Description ==

Introducing the WooCommerce Product Accordion, a powerful tool designed to enhance the presentation and user experience of your online store. This innovative plugin seamlessly integrates with your WooCommerce platform, allowing you to organize and showcase your products in a sleek, accordion-style format.

With the WooCommerce Product Accordion, you can create dynamic product displays that are both visually appealing and user-friendly. Customers can easily navigate through categories and subcategories, expanding or collapsing sections to view products of interest without leaving the main page.
